Love of my life

  1. In the vastness of a starlit night,
    A love was born, shining so bright.
    Her smile, a constellation's grace,
    I'm captivated by her celestial face.


  2. Her eyes, like galaxies, deep and vast,
    Hold secrets and dreams that forever.
    In her touch, I feel the gentle breeze,
    Whispering melodies that put my heart at ease.


  3. Her laughter, a symphony of pure delight,
    Filling my world with colors so bright.
    Her voice, a melody that soothes my soul,
    Guiding me towards love's eternal goal.


  4. With every step, she paints a poetic verse,
    A masterpiece that my heart can't rehearse.
    Her presence, a muse that ignites the fire,
    Inspiring words that my soul aspires.


  5. In her embrace, I find solace and peace,
    A sanctuary where all worries cease.
    She's the rhythm that dances within my veins,
    A love story that forever remains.


  6. So, to my dearest, my love, my muse,
    These words I offer, forever to choose.
    You're the poetry that fills my world,
    And with you, my love, my heart is unfurled.
